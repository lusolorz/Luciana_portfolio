What is Plot web?


PlotWeb is an app designed to help writers organize their characters and visualize relationships between them.
On the left is MVP 1, which my team and I developed and debugged using the Flutter SDK, transforming our idea into a functional application. Beyond coding, we followed the entire product life cycle, ensuring a structured and well-rounded development process.

Project Timeline

Research


Research To ensure our project aligned with the needs of creative writers, our team conducted research by engaging directly with them. We asked what aspects of the writing process they found most challenging and what could be automated without compromising content or creativity. The most common challenge they highlighted was avoiding plot holes and maintaining a cohesive plot structure.
