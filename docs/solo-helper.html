My sister, a chemistry teacher, recently approached me with a problem she had been facing. At the end of every semester, she was required to determine which students would receive detentions or warnings depending on their absences (excused, unexcused, and late). Although this can be done on excel, most of her fellow teachers were not aware or familiar with excels high-level functionality and simply manually checked their absences to determine which students would receive detentions. I decided I would address this problem by creating a webapp with a login that would function much like excel but only apply to teaching related functionality, like grading based on curves, inputting grades, and even using a combination of LaTeX and an AI Hugging face model to help generate tests for teachers. Although these functionalities will probably be implemented later I have decided to begin with the frontend for the absences functionality. My next step will be to host the project with Amazon Lambda and use python for data processing.
